Our Solution

BrainVu uses a non-invasive and remote mobile/AR/VR camera to extract physiological bio-markers that indicate changes in brain activities and extract human emotional responses including: Mental Load, Stress Level and Emotional Engagement. Using these responses BrainVu can connect to a multitude of platforms and provide emotional and mental representation for those responses.
